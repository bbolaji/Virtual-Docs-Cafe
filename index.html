About Us

Virtual Doc Café is an innovative online platform designed to replicate the concept of a traditional café where people can perform various document-related tasks. Our services include document editing, CV drafting, third-party insurance processing, and auto registration, photocopying, and printing. Our target audience includes students, job seekers, small business owners, and freelancers. Our mission is to provide a convenient, secure, and comprehensive solution for all document management needs.
